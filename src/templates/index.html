Commissioning

If you want some software made for you, you can commission me. Message me via email, or preferably, matrix to do so. The software will be released under some sort of GPL license but it is not required that you distribute it. You, as the user or commissioner, have the right to use and distribute the software as you wish given those who you distribute it also follow the rules of the GPL. You may even choose to keep the software secret and only use it for yourself. All I ask is that people who get the software know who made it and that they don't infringe on the rights of others to use the same software if they distribute it.

You may pay me in bitcoin, or preferably monero. I will need more time and money to do bigger projects, obviously. Please note that I have a limited skillset in terms of programming, however if you want something sort of simple done I can do that.
